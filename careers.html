PIT History

For more than few years, PIT has been experiencing positive growth on a global scale. Through it all, it's our people — customers, business partners and team members — who keep us moving forward through the rapidly changing market. We have built a solid, worldwide PIT community. We focus more on the people, care about the daily lives of our team members, whose contributions make our whole group to reach the goal. Our philosophy toward people is to "build enduring relationships with both external and internal team members." The focus in PIT for our team members is mainly on creating an environment where every individual can be their best and do their best work in service of our customers.

PIT Culture

We always adhere to the people-oriented, integrity and innovative culture, for well providing diverse and challenging work environment to our employees, and brings their best qualities to the fore. We value individuals with “can-do” mentality who are ambitious and innovative in their thinking and focus on learning and development to build personal capabilities We recognize and reward high-performing employees, operating on a “total remuneration” basis that is competitive, market-based and encourages exceptional performance by individuals, teams and the whole company. We celebrate the success of high-performing employees through personal development awards, annual salary reviews and short-term incentive program.

Our Value We are all writing PIT’S story. As individuals, we are shaped by different cultural, professional and personal experiences. Our shared values enable us to combine our personal strengths with those of others to make PIT stronger, better, more innovative and more successful. We could write a novel about our values. We’re a company that spans many countries and cultures, and values are hugely important to us. But when you boil it down, there are just four simple things that guide us. And it doesn’t matter who or where we are within PIT, there are values we share. Co-operative Spirit Our co-operative spirit means we put the whole of PIT before any individual part. It means we all pitch in, volunteering our knowledge, capabilities and personal networks. We always put safety first for both ourselves and others. We form lasting partnerships. We respect what is important to our communities and our actions promote and enhance our reputation while honoring our heritage. Do what’s Right Doing what’s right means maintaining the highest ethical standards at all times. It is often simply doing what we say we will. It’s about speaking openly and being prepared to have the tough conversations. It’s about treating others as we expect them to treat us. And it’s about having the courage to speak up and challenge when things just don’t seem right.
 
Challenge Boundaries Challenging boundaries will keep us one step ahead. It means we look at the future through customer’s eyes. It also means we’ll never sit still; we’ll always be lifting standards and improving quality. It’s about inventing, improving and solving. And it means learning from our successes and our mistakes. Make it happen Making it happen is about creating a climate for success. We aim high to deliver exceptional results. We step up and we take accountability. We plan thoroughly and we persevere. And then when we achieve, we celebrate success.

Our leaders emerge through mentorship, encouragement and opportunities that blend rewarding and challenging together. From one-to-one meetings with mentors to cross check the regular review feedback and routine track with a long-term career plan, our team members have ample opportunity to achieve their goals

Learning at PIT:
We anticipate cultivating the confidence, drive and abilities in all of our team members, which empower them to fulfill their potential.
