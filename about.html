About CPG
HOME > ABOUT CPG

Capital Power Global Limited (CPG) is not only a large global online entertainment operator, but also a leading global online gaming solution provider.

For more than a few years, CPG has become one of the world largest online gaming companies, with a large number of online gaming websites. As the world’s most professional online entertainment operator, CPG has world-class consultants. With their research in gaming for over 30 years, CPG has accumulated rich experience in marketing, advanced technology forces to form a competent organizational system..

Being one of leading companies in the design and development of solution with the most advanced technology, we offer versatile gaming solutions and customization services successfully for gaming industry. With the best team and rich experience, we fulfill customers' diverse demands and shorten customers' product development cycle and help customers speed up the “Time to Market” to gain a competitive edge.Through our dedication to customer-centric innovation and strong partnerships, we have established end-to-end capabilities and strengths. We are committed to creating maximum value for customers by providing competitive gaming solutions and services. Our products and solutions have been deployed in many countries, serving a lot of clients.

To adapt to the rapid changes that are taking place in the online gaming industry, CPG is making adjustments to better service its customers. CPG will remain focus on customers’ needs as we continue to innovate, openly cooperate with partners, and deepen our commitment to providing integrated solutions, products and services. We will also continue to enhance the users experience, create maximum value for our customers, and improve work efficiency through effective communication.

With comprehensive capabilities, incomparable experience, and extensive professional research on the gaming industry, CPG has worked together with customers that help them do and achieve more. CGP delivers the most comprehensive, most timely and accurate professional solutions, and a full range of international service which make our customers perform at the highest levels so that they can create sustainable value for their clients and shareholders. Breakthrough in the advanced technology and constant innovation concept, in line with market change, and develop solutions to help customers to adapt to changes, launch new business, and improve operational performance more effectively and efficiently.
